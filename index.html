Daily Rosary

Read and reflect on the mysteries of the day.

How to Pray the Rosary

1.

Make the Sign of the Cross.

2.

Holding the Crucifix, say the Apostles' Creed.

3.

On the first bead, say an Our Father.

4.

Say one Hail Mary on each of the next three beads.

5.

Say the Glory Be.

6.

For each of the five decades, announce the Mystery then say the Our Father.

7.

While fingering each of the ten beads, say ten Hail Marys. Then say a Glory Be.

(Fatima Prayer: O My Jesus, forgive us our sins, save us from the fires of Hell and lead all souls to Heaven, especially those who are in most need of Thy mercy.)

8.

After five decades, say the Hail, Holy Queen.

Concluding Prayers

Hail, Holy Queen

Hail, Holy Queen, Mother of Mercy, our life, our sweetness and our hope. To thee do we cry, poor banished children of Eve: to thee do we send up our sighs, mourning and weeping in this valley of tears. Turn then, most gracious Advocate, thine eyes of mercy toward us, and after this our exile, show unto us the blessed fruit of thy womb, Jesus. O clement, O loving, O sweet Virgin Mary!

V. Pray for us, O holy Mother of God.

R. That we may be made worthy of the promises of Christ.

Let us pray

O God, whose Only Begotten Son, by his life, Death, and Resurrection, has purchased for us the rewards of eternal life, grant, we beseech thee, that while meditating on these mysteries of the most holy Rosary of the Blessed Virgin Mary, we may imitate what they contain and obtain what they promise, through the same Christ our Lord. Amen.

The Presence of Christ

Within its sacred walls—especially in St. Peter’s Basilica and the Sistine Chapel—countless prayers and sacred rituals are offered in remembrance of His message of love, forgiveness, and salvation. The Vatican preserves, reflects, and shares the teachings of Jesus, guiding millions of believers around the world in their faith.

In this way, Vatican City is not only a political and religious center, but also a living symbol of Christ’s enduring presence within the traditions and spiritual life of the Church.
